Dissecting Unicorns

There are a number of ways that you can become a data scientist. Depending on your background and preferred career trajectory, a PhD might be important, or perhaps a bootcamp is more up your alley. Whatever your path, the goal of this web app is to reveal the myriad ways that people get into this field and lower the barrier to entry for prospective data scientists.

This project collates anonymous data from its participants' LinkedIn profiles to capture previous jobs they have held, educational experience, and skills. The goal is to make it possible for other people to see the potential of a career in data science. If you are interested in participating in this project, please click below to allow us to store, aggregate, and analyze your career trajectory so that we can tell our collective story to others.
